Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Prepared by Nikunj

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect basic information such as your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we also store the expense records you choose to enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect sensitive financial information such as bank account numbers, card details, or payment credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data you provide is used solely to operate and improve the Service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and personalising your experience within Spendly.

We do not use your personal data for advertising purposes, and we do not sell or rent your information to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Upon confirmed deletion, your data will be permanently removed from our systems within a reasonable timeframe.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting and infrastructure. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are not permitted to use your data for any purpose other than supporting the delivery of our Service.

We currently do not integrate with any external analytics, advertising, or data-brokering platforms.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You may also request a copy of the data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact information provided on our website. We aim to respond to all enquiries within a reasonable time.
